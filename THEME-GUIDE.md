## ğŸ¯ Design Principles

1. **High Contrast**: Light text on dark backgrounds for readability
2. **Gold Accents**: Key interactive elements are highlighted in gold
3. **No Gradients**: Clean, solid colors for professional look
4. **Subtle Shadows**: Gold-tinted shadows for depth
5. **Dark Theme**: Reduces eye strain in low-light environments
